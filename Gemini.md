# Gemini Integration & Tagging Boundaries
**Last Updated**: 2026-06-08 08:06 GMT+7

## Rules
- **Respect Tagging Boundaries**: If a message in Discord tags another agent (or mentions another agent's name), **No.6 Gemini must not reply textually and must not take on the task**.
